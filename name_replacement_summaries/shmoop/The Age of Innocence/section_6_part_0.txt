Mr. van der Luyden stands on the principle that if Countess Olenska is backed up by her family, no one has a right to snub her. Mr. and Mrs. van der Luyden decide to invite Countess Olenska to a dinner at their place to meet their cousin, the Duke of St. Austrey. Such an invitation is a high honor and would cement her position in society.
